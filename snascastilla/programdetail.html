Program Offerings

Computer System Servicing NC II

Job Description

Consists of competencies that must possess to enable to install and configure computers systems, set-up computer networks and servers and to maintain and repair computer systems and networks.

Entry Requirements

Can communicate either oral and written and must pass the trainability/aptitude test.

Job Opportunities

A person who has achieved this Qualification is competent to be a:

  • COMPUTER ASSEMBLER
  • COMPUTER SERVICE TECHNICIAN
  • NETWORK TECHNICIAN
  • COMPUTER MAINTENANCE TECHNICIAN
Evidences

Upon completion of the Course, the trainee/student must be able to:

  • Install and configure computer systems
  • Set-up Computer Networks
  • Set-up Computer Servers
  • Maintain and Repair Computer Systems and Networks
National Assessment

To attain the National Qualification of Computer Systems Servicing NC II, the candidate must demonstrate competence through project-type assessment covering all units. Successful candidates shall be awarded a National Certificate signed by the TESDA Director General.
